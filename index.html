// Craft

A compute model for all garments.

Every piece is pattern-cut, shadow-dyed, and finished by hand. The supply chain is as deliberate as the design.

Materials

Japanese coated cotton, brushed wool-cashmere, sandwashed silk charmeuse.

Every fabric is sourced for drape, hand-feel, and durability — never for cost.

Fit

Silhouettes engineered from the shoulder down. Oversized, tailored, draped.

Each cut is calibrated to the fabric — the drape informs the shape.

Construction

Blind seams, structured shoulders, integrated pockets. Function disappears into form.

The details are invisible until you wear the piece. That is the point.
